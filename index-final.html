About Nordic Barista Cup

The vision within the Nordic Barista Cup is:

"To create an environment in which knowledge about coffee and its sphere can be obtained."

'...create an environment...'

Combined with personally absorption having the opportunity to see and experience countries, people, traditions etc, will alwayas serve as a source of inspiration in out daily work. The organozaton behind the Nordic Barista Cup see it as its main purpose to be a part of creating this forum in which people can meet, bond and achieve further knowledge
